(**) Adding Squared Numbers In The Form: a2 + (3a)2:

   A.  This method is very easy since from algebra we know:

a2 + (3a)2 = 10a2

   B.  Therefore, you square the smaller number, then multiply by 10 for the answer.

Ex [1]  322 + 962 =_________.

a)      322 = 1024.  See Squaring Numbers.

b)      1024 x 10 = 10240.

c)      The answer is 10240.

Ex [2]  492 + 1472 =_________.

a)      492 = 2401. See Squaring Numbers in the Range: 40 - 49.

b)      2401 x 10 = 24010.

c)  The answer is 24010.
