Multiplying By 286:

   A.  This method is derived from Multiplying by 143.  Since 286 = 1001 x (2/7) you can divide by 7, multiply by 2, then multiply by 1001.

 Ex [1]  77 x 286 =_________.

a)      77 ÷ 7 = 11.

b)      11 x 2 = 22.

c)      22 x 1001 = 22022.

d)      The answer is 22022.

Ex [2]  286 x 287 =_________.

a)      287 ÷ 7 = 41.

b)      41 x 2 = 82.

c)      82 x 1001 = 82082.

d)      The answer is 82082.
