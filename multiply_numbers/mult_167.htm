Multiplying By 167:

   A.  This method uses alternate ways of Multiplying by 1001 because 167 = 1002 ÷ 6.  So you must divide by 6 and multiply by 1002.

Ex [1]  72 x 167 =_________.

a)      72 ÷ 6 = 12.

b)      12 x 1002 = 12024.

c)      The answer is 12024.

Ex [2]  732 x 167 =_________.

a)      732 ÷ 6 = 122.

b)      122 x 1002 = 122244.

c)  The answer is 122244.
