Multiplying By 143

   A.  To use this method you will need to be familiar with Multiplying by 1001.

1.  Since 143 = 1001 ÷ 7 this method is simple.  Simply divide by 7 and multiply by 1001.

Ex [1]  84 x 143 =_________.

a)      84 ÷ 7 = 12.

b)      12 x 101 = 12012.

c)      The answer is 12012.

Ex [2]  147 x 143 =_________.

a)      147 ÷ 7 = 21.

b)      21 x 101 = 21021.

c)      The answer is 21021.
