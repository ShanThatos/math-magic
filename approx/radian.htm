Approximating Changing Radians To Degrees To Radians:

A.  In general:

1 radian 57 degrees

1 degree .017 radians

B.  Examples:

Ex [1]  5 radians = ________ degrees

a.  57 x 5 = 285.

b.  The answer can be between 273 - 300.

Ex [2]  17000 degrees = _______ radians

a.  17000 x .017 = 289. 

b.  The answer can be between 282 - 311.
