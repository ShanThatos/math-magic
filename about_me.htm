Hello,  My name is Dewayne Jones.  You may be asking yourself, "What credibility does he have in creating a site about Number Sense?"  

Well, in 1997 I was a state champion in the 2AA division.  I have been in Number Sense since I was in the fourth grade and since graduating from high school, I have always been afraid of losing all of the knowledge that I have worked so hard to learn. Even though I have not even seen a Number Sense test since graduating, I have always been debating about creating a web site or writing a book.  So finally, I decided to build this long overdue site.  I hope that you find this site helpful and informative.  If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions, feel free to email me.
